On 26 February 2019 Indian Air Force Fighter jets had cross the LOC.PAF Fighter Planes tried to intercept but IAF Fighter jets ran back to their country. On the next day, 27 February 2019, the sun rises for PAF Shaheen's. PAF Shot down IAF two Fighter jets including MIG-21 and Su-30.IAF Pilot Wing Commander Abhinandan was captured by PAF.This day is celebrated as PAF surprise day."WE ARE PROUD OF OUR SHAHEENS".

Pakistani pilots who shot down IAF planes:

  • Wing Commander Noman Ali
  • Squadron Leader Hassaan Siddiqui

Indian Pilot Captured by PAF:
